2. Usage

Join a Teams meeting with transcription on, then click the bookmarklet. A dialog will appear to start or resume a capture session.

To export from the console at any time:

copy(JSON.stringify(window.transcript, null, 2))

To stop the observer:

window.myObserver.disconnect()

Development Mode

Start local server in the checked out repo

python3 -m http.server 9985

Then open http://localhost:9985 to get a bookmarklet built from your local script.js.
